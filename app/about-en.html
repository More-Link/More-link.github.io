    COMPANY PROFILE

    More-Link Technology Co., Ltd. Is a (wired & wireless) router supplier and solution company. "More-Link" sounds like "Morning", it’s a symbol of our passional creative and positive attitudes will lead us to a sunshine future.

    Over 12 years of continuous R&D in wired & wireless routers.

    More than 20 senior Hardware & Software engineers, the truth of the long-term development of enterprises are based on the strong scientific experiments and marketing research.

    Developed PandaoraBox OS., which is a famous and high-performance routers’ system.

    Experienced 5 years in deep developing in wired and wireless router, from DreamBox to today’s PandoraBox System.

    Long and stable corporation.
